# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Use inclusive language
- Be respectful of different viewpoints
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on collaboration
- Show empathy

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or threats
- Offensive comments about identity or background
- Publishing private information without consent
- Spam or unwanted promotion
- Any behavior that's inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers directly. All reports are kept confidential and will be reviewed fairly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code, and to temporarily or permanently ban violators.
